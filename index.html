About Me

My Story

Dmitry Shamis is a web developer from New York City looking to build the next great piece of technology. As a recent gradaute of General Assembly's twelve-week Web Development Immersive program, Dmitry is able to program in Ruby (Rails, Sinatra) and Javascript, and is eager to continue expanding his knowledge base.

An NYU graduate and former talent agent, Dmitry has worked with Paradigm Talent Agency, Universal Music Group, and Spike TV among others. Dmitry lives on the Upper East Side of Manhattan with his fiance and their Mini Schnauzer. In his spare time, Dmitry enjoys cooking, reading, PS4, and playing soccer with his Chivas teammates at Pier 5.
